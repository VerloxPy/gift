Fast_Blast is a Telegram e-commerce assistant bot developed in Python. The bot was built for an online store to streamline the ordering process and improve customer interaction.

With Fast_Blast, users can easily:

  • Browse available products
  • View discounts and special offers
  • Place orders for selected items
  • Submit delivery information directly inside Telegram
  • Receive order confirmations from the system

The bot integrates an intuitive step-by-step flow, making the purchasing experience simple and user-friendly. It was implemented using the python-telegram-bot library and follows clean, modular architecture principles, ensuring maintainability and scalability for future upgrades.

Fast_Blast demonstrates efficient automation of online store operations by reducing manual processing and enabling direct product ordering through a messenger platform.
